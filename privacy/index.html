Sheetsync Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

What data we access

Sheetsync reads M-Pesa SMS messages from your device inbox to detect and record your mobile money transactions. No other SMS messages are read.

Where data is stored

Transactions are stored locally on your device in an encrypted database. No data is sent to our servers.

Third-party sharing

If you connect a Smartsheet account, your transaction data is synced to your own Smartsheet sheet at your direction. We have no access to your Smartsheet account or data.

Data deletion

You can delete all locally stored data at any time using the "Reset All Data" option in the app's Settings screen.
